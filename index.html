About Me

I am an agile driven and passionate product manager with years of experience in executing ideas. From:

  • Raising money for my student exchange startup in college.
  • Starting a college news site from zero and growing it to an audience of 15k with 2-3k monthly active readers in 3 months.
  • Running a network of Facebook meme pages that reach an audience of 5 million likes generating $15k/month from ads at its peak.
  • Launching an anonymous college confessions website and grow the Facebook audience to 45k students then selling it to a San Francisco startup.
  • Launching a student social network mobile app that reached 50k users.
  • Starting a development shop and launching 20+ websites and mobile apps.

To solving user problems for large and small companies. I am extremely user focused and work tirelessly to turn user problems into closed tickets.
